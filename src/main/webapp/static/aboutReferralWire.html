A Partner Network Built on Common Goals

ReferralWire connects you to other businesses that serve the same clientele. These businesses have customers who need your services. ReferralWire helps you transform these businesses into your most productive sales channel.

Why Networking will Always Be More Powerful than Advertising

Most professionals work in a crowded market. A customer's decision to contract with you instead of someone else is often simply a matter of trust. Paid advertising helps familiarize your prospects with who you are. Consumer reviews and endorsements from social web sites may help your credibility. However none can inspire trust like a referral from an expert who knows the prospect.

Effective networkers encourage other businesses to give them referrals by doing the same in return. However most professionals only do so sporadically. ReferralWire makes it easy for members to systematically and efficiently network with other businesses to produce a steady stream of quality referrals.

Interactive Sales Tools on the Desktop, Tablet and Smartphone

In order to develop an effective partner ecosystem, it's essential that you make it easy for other businesses to deliver your sales message. Unfortunately it's often difficult for other businesses to remember your pitch when the time comes.

ReferralWire puts your profile onto your partners' desktop, tablet and smartphones in a way that is:
  • Easy to find -- Other businesses can find you by typing in a few letters of your name, your business name or key words you've chosen.
  • Easy to read -- Your profile summarizes the services you offer, the customers you serve, and your qualifications. It doesn't include extraneous information about your personal work history and isn't cluttered with advertisements and other distractions.
  • Visually compelling -- Your profile can include slides, logos, pictures, charts and other images that enable partners to show as well as tell your story.

Get the Right Information to Qualify Prospects

Referrals passed on a slip of paper or by way of email often lack the key information needed for you to qualify a prospect. In most cases, the source of the referral isn't to blame. He or she often doesn't know what specific information matters to you.

ReferralWire helps ensure your partners include the details you've asked for when they give you a referral. Business partners are presented with a short form that includes your custom questions. The form can be filled out in a few seconds from a smartphone, tablet or desktop. Armed with the right information, you can now quickly distinguish promising prospects from lower priority leads -- and make a good impression when you make the first call.

Build Trust Through Feedback

Strong, reciprocal referral networks are built on a foundation of trust. Historically this trust has been limited to small groups of intimate colleagues.

ReferralWire enables you to increase the scope of your partner network without compromising trust. It discretely provides information on the outcome of each referral that you give to a partner -- based on feedback from both your partner and the prospect.
