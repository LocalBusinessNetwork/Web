ReferralWire Privacy Policy

  1. Personal information collected

    We collect information:

    • When you register an account to become a ReferralWire user (“User”), such as your name, e-mail, employer, country, and a password.
    • When you view and interact with ReferralWire pages, features, and functionality, including ReferralWire mobile applications, software (like adding to your profile, uploading contacts, etc.), and platform technology. We also collect your IP address, browser type, operating system, mobile carrier, and your ISP, and receive the URLs of sites from which you arrive or leave the ReferralWire website, or sites that have embedded ReferralWire platform technology.
    • Through cookies and other technologies that allow us to recognize you and customize your experience. Learn more about cookies, beacons in Sections 1G and 1H, below.

  2. Uses of personal information

    We use the information you provide to:

    • Enable you to share your information and communicate with other Users, or provide your personal details to third parties offering combined services with ReferralWire;
    • Administer your account with us and customize the service we provide to you and other Users; and to
    • Send you service or promotional communications through email and notices on the ReferralWire website.
    • Connect you to opportunities by enabling other professionals to find you on ReferralWire.

    We do not sell, rent, or otherwise provide personally identifiable information to third parties without your consent except where it is necessary to carry out your instructions (to process your payment information, for example) or as described in Section 2 of this Privacy Policy. Also, we may share information.

    Please note that in order to fulfill your requests for paid services, we share your payment information with our payment processor.

  3. Your Information Choices

    You can:

    • Review, enhance or edit your personal information through your personal profile page;
    • Accept or Ignore Referrals from your partners
    • Change your password
    • Tell us to close your ReferralWire

  4. Your obligations to other Users.

    • Do not abuse the ReferralWire service by using it to spam, abuse, harass, or otherwise violate the User Agreement or Privacy Policy.

  5. Security

    • Personal information you provide will be secured in accordance with industry standards and technology. Since the internet is not a 100% secure environment, we cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information you transmit to ReferralWire. There is no guarantee that information may not be accessed, copied, disclosed, altered, or destroyed by breach of any of our physical, technical, or managerial safeguards.
    • You are responsible for maintaining the secrecy of your unique password and account information, and for controlling access to your email communications at all times.

  6. How to contact us

    If you have questions or comments please send them to us at support@referralwire.com
