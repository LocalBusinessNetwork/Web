This view shows a list of all referrals that you have received and are actively working on.

Accepting a prospect referral shows your partner you have acknowledged the referral and allows you to see information about the prospect.

Accepting a power parter referral or invitation indicates that you agree to be connected with the other party.

Confirming a previously accepted referral signifies to your partner that the prospect is interested and creates your own personal copy of the contact record

Converting a previously confirmed referral signifies to your partner that you have signed the prospect on as a new client. It also archives the referral

Archiving a referral moves it out of your received list because you're no longer actively working on it. You can see a list of archived referrals in the Archived view.

New referrals can be created by clicking "Make a referral! on the home page.

This view shows contact details for the prospect as well as other publicly available information about them from LinkedIn.

You must accept popups from ReferralWire in order to see the LinkedIn information. Log in under your own LinkedIn user name and password when prompted.

The list of LinkedIn user profiles is limited to those with whom you have at least a second degree relationship.

LinkedIn also restricts the number of contacts you can view through this interface in a 24 hour period. If you wish to view a large number referral prospects on LinkedIn, please access the LinkedIn site directly.
